CHANEL DE BLEU — YUTARO GOTO

The Chanel de Bleu campaign explores fragrance as atmosphere rather than product. Through controlled natural lighting and restrained composition, the imagery builds a quiet emotional distance.

It reflects identity through stillness, texture, and cinematic simplicity.

VOGUE — JACOB ELORDI

This Vogue feature presents a minimalist study of modern masculinity through structured editorial framing and reduced visual noise.

The emphasis is placed on posture, silhouette, and controlled presence.
