A queer horror narrative exploring identity, repression, and the distortion of self within systems of fear, faith, and desire.

About the Film

Leviticus is an upcoming experimental horror project rooted in psychological tension and queer identity. It blends religious symbolism, fragmented memory, and surreal visual storytelling to explore internal fear as a physical space.
