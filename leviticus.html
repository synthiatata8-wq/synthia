A SYNTHIA FILM FEATURE

What begins as a controlled press moment between Stacey Clausen and Joe Bird slowly dissolves into something less stable — where performance begins to leak into identity.

This is not a story that explains itself. It unfolds through observation, distance, and fractured perception.
